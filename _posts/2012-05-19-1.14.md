title: An easy conjugate computation
layout: default
---
Recall that $|z|^2 = z\overline{z}$. Then
$$\begin{aligned}
|1 + z|^2 + |1 - z|^2 &= (1 + z)(1 + \overline{z}) + (1 - z)(1 - \overline{z}) \\\\
                      &= 1 + \overline{z} + z + z\overline{z} + 1 - \overline{z} - z + z\overline{z} \\\\
                      &= 4
\end{aligned}$$
